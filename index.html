My fav books

  1. Heidi

    By Johanna Spyri

    An orphaned girl moves to the Swiss Alps to live with her grandfather. The neighbors are concerned because he is a bitter old man. Her personality soon softens his heart and changes him. She makes alot of friends with her neighbors as she settles in the countryside.

  2. The Pirate's Coin

    By Cassandra Campbell

    Two students find a magical key that allow them to shrink into the Thorne Rooms inside the Chicago Institute of Art Museum. Opening a door takes them into a different time and place setting they can experience.

    3. Barbie:Rapunzel

    By Cliff Ruby

    Rapunzel is locked in a tower by an evil witch named Gothel. She discovers a chest of magical paintbrushes and a hairbrush. It allows her to exit the tower and finally visit the village, where she learns secrets about her parents.
